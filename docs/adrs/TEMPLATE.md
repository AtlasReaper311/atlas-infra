# ADR-NNNN: short imperative title

## Context

What forced the decision. The problem, the constraints, the evidence. Enough that a reader who was not there understands why a choice was needed, without yet knowing which way it went.

## Decision

The choice, stated in the present tense as a rule the estate now follows. One decision per record.

## Consequences

What this makes true, good and bad. The new guarantees, the new obligations, the things that now have to be remembered. An honest record names the costs, not only the wins.
